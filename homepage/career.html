Prism Infosec

A week at Prism Infosec during the summer of 2022, taught me the OWASP top 10 framework where I learnt the risk, exploitation and mitigation of each vulnerability. I later applied the knowledge by finding vulnerabilities in a webapp using nmap and burp suite, allowing me to gain experience with real cybersecurity problems. I also used GoPhish to spear phish an employee highlighting the importance of having strong email security. Overall, the week helped expand my penetration testing skills and to understand how cybersecurity concepts and theories can be applied in practice.
